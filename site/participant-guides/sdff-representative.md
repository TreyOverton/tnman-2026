## What this role is

SFC Acosta serves the TF in **three parallel capacities**, deliberately and explicitly:

1. **SDFF Agency Representative** ([ICS-203 §4](/docs/ics-203.md)) — direct liaison line to the IC for SDFF integration. SDFF is providing **5 Part 107 pilot volunteers for E. TN / Holston** (and 4 for the Fall Creek Falls mapping mission). See [Volunteer Pilots](/volunteer-pilots.md).
2. **Pilot 3 in the Air Ops Branch** ([ICS-203 §7](/docs/ics-203.md)) — flying alongside CPT Widner (Pilot 1) and 1LT Riley (Pilot 2) under CPT Borrilez (Air Ops Branch Director).
3. **Member of the 6 MAY 1455R Leader's Recon team** — already met Mr. Armstrong before any drone tasking.

The Agency Rep role is **liaison and integration**, not command. The Agency Rep:

- **Speaks for SDFF** on SDFF integration questions
- **Translates** between the 3 RGT TF chain and the SDFF organization
- **Routes** SDFF questions and TF requirements through the right people on each side
- Per LTC Estes' 27 APR direction: *"I'd like you and your team to operate as part of TNSG as this part of the exercise. Doing so will avoid confusion."*

## Rank vs role

This is a complex rank-vs-role billet because of the parallel capacities and the cross-organization integration:

- **You are TNSG IRR** — Inactive Ready Reserve — operating as a SFC on this exercise. You are not in the active 3 RGT chain of command, but you are **operating as part of TNSG** under LTC Estes' direction for this exercise.
- **As Agency Representative**, your authority is the role — you can engage the IC directly on SDFF integration questions. You are not a subordinate within the Operations Section for this purpose.
- **As Pilot 3**, you report to CPT Borrilez within the Air Ops Branch — same expectation as any other pilot. Pilot in Command authority for your aircraft is the certificate; Branch Director authority over flight pattern, zone, and timing comes through Borrilez.
- **SDFF is an adjacent unit, not subject to Military Department rules** — but for this exercise you operate as TNSG. The distinction matters for the [JAG Pre-Brief — DJI Personal Use](/docs/jag-brief-dji.md) analysis: SDFF airframes flown in TNSG capacity may trigger different statutory considerations depending on funding nexus.
- **You're senior in age and operational experience** to several of the participants, including some officers. Defer to ICS role assignment in operational direction; carry your experience in mentorship and informal guidance.

## Out of scope (do NOT do)

- **Do not commit SDFF to TF taskings** beyond what's in the [Volunteer Pilots](/volunteer-pilots.md) coordination. Major SDFF asks route through the IC.
- **Do not engage Mr. Armstrong directly on drone tasking** without coordinating with LTC Sheaf first (per Estes' 27 APR direction).
- **Do not fly DJI airframes** pending JAG resolution of the [DJI Pre-Brief](/docs/jag-brief-dji.md) — coordinate with CPT Borrilez and the IC if any SDFF pilot brings a DJI airframe. JAG response is pending.
- **Do not act as Liaison Officer** for non-SDFF external organizations — that's the TF Liaison Officer.
- **Do not run SECFOR or any Branch outside Air Ops** — your role is Agency Rep + Pilot 3.
