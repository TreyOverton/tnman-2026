## What this role is

Technical Specialists provide **subject-matter expertise** to the Planning Section on demand. Doctrinally a Technical Specialist is whatever the incident needs that the standard ICS structure doesn't cover. On this TF:

- **SSG Miles — Technical Specialist (SECFOR)**: SECFOR experience and MP-track perspective. Advises the Section on screen-line tasks, civilian-interaction posture, and MP-track best practices. The Section can also tap him for tabletop-patrol perspective.
- **WO1 Hendon — Technical Specialist (general)**: Warrant Officer specialty perspective. Brought in for any specific input needed during planning meetings or AAR collation. Advises on personnel-record integrity (4 BN BN S1 NCO primary duty).

The role is **advisory and on-call**, not continuous. You're available to the Section, called when needed, otherwise contributing to ICS-214 collection and AAR observation capture like everyone else.

## Rank vs role

Two distinct rank dynamics in this billet:

- **SSG and WO1 are different communities** — NCO and Warrant Officer respectively — both serving the same role of "expert called on demand." Neither outranks the other in this billet; both report to 1LT Overton.
- **Technical Specialists are doctrinal-equal advisors** regardless of rank. A junior NCO or a Warrant Officer specialist can advise an LTC Section Chief or the IC without the rank distance compromising the input. The role exists precisely because incident-relevant expertise doesn't always live at officer rank.
- **You both report to 1LT Overton** within the Section — Section Chief authority runs through the role.
- **MRC verifications pending** — Hendon MRC-4, Miles MRC-3 confirmed (single-day 14 MAY). See [Roster Open Items](../roster.md) and [DD 2977 Block 4](../docs/dd2977.md).
