## What this role is

A Part 107 Pilot operates a small unmanned aircraft system (sUAS, < 55 lb) under FAA 14 CFR Part 107 in support of the exercise:

- **Pilot in Command** for each flight — final authority on go/no-go, altitude, return-to-home
- Operates within the **blue-oval flight zone** on [Ops Graphics](../docs/ops-graphics.md) slide 4
- Flies in support of the **HAAP sUAS detection system** — detection is the chief HAAP training objective (Mr. Armstrong 20 APR)
- Maintains **Part 107 compliance** — line of sight, max 400 ft AGL, max 100 mph, registration, certificate carry
- **Logs the flight** per Part 107 record-keeping and per ICS-214

## Rank vs role

The three pilots are **CPT, 1LT, and SFC respectively** — all serving the same role with equal Pilot-in-Command authority for their respective flights:

- **Pilot in Command authority is the certificate, not the rank.** A SFC PIC has the same go/no-go authority over their aircraft as a CPT PIC does. The certificate is what the FAA recognizes.
- **All three pilots report to CPT Borrilez** as Branch Director within the Air Ops Branch — the rank-line and role-line align cleanly here.
- **CPT Widner is also 4 BN XO**, **1LT Riley is also 3 BN XO**, **SFC Acosta is TNSG IRR and SDFF Agency Rep**. Dual-hats exist but don't conflict during flight ops.
- **SFC Acosta has a separate liaison line** to the IC as the SDFF Agency Representative ([ICS-203 §4](../docs/ics-203.md)) — he can engage the IC directly on SDFF integration questions outside the Air Ops chain.

## Out of scope (do NOT do)

- **DO NOT** fly outside the blue-oval flight zone or across the NO-GO Line.
- **DO NOT** capture imagery within the HAAP fence (LTC Sheaf 11 APR; TCA § 39-13-903).
- **DO NOT** fly without your Part 107 certificate on person and the aircraft registered.
- **DO NOT** fly DJI airframes pending JAG resolution of the [DJI Pre-Brief](../docs/jag-brief-dji.md) — coordinate with CPT Borrilez and the IC if you're bringing a DJI airframe.
- **DO NOT** extend the flight beyond the scheduled window without Branch Director approval.
- **DO NOT** engage the HAAP detection system directly — your job is to fly the pattern; the detection coordination runs through the IC / Liaison.
