| Field | Value |

| **Section** | Operations / SECFOR Branch |
| **Operational period** | 13-15 MAY 26 |

## What this role is

The Screen Division/Group Leader runs the **screen-line element** of the SECFOR Branch — three positions in doctrine, of which **one is "almost actual" (a 3-PAX foot patrol)** along the southernmost side of US-11W and **two are notional** (tabletop in the ICP). The role:

- Owns **screen-line tasking** and tabletop execution of the two notional patrols
- **Briefs the foot patrol** before release on position, NO-GO Line, civilian posture, hydration, comms
- **Tracks observations** from the foot patrol and from the notional-patrol tabletop work; feeds them to the Situation Unit and SECFOR Branch
- Coordinates with the **Air Ops Branch** on any sUAS sighting / observation

## Expectations during TNMAN-26

### 13 MAY

- Coordinate with **SFC Ferguson** (acting Branch Director on 13 MAY) on the foot patrol's 14 MAY brief.
- Set up **tabletop materials** for the two notional patrols — large-format Ops Graphics, NAI list, comms callouts.
- Verify the foot patrol element has CalTopo, water, ICS-214, ICS-213 forms.

### 14 MAY

- **Brief the foot patrol** at 0900 before release; verify SSG Whalen and team understand position, NO-GO Line, civilian posture.
- **Run the two notional patrols** as tabletop activities — position changes, NAI checks, comms checks — at the same cadence the foot patrol is operating.
- During the **1300 OPFOR main-gate event** and **1400-1430 sUAS event**, escalate any observed activity to MAJ Crosby and the Situation Unit.
- Coordinate with the **Comms Unit** on radio traffic from the foot patrol.

### 15 MAY

- Capture screen-line AAR observations.
- Hand tabletop materials to the Documentation Unit.

## Rank vs role

2LT is appropriate rank for a Division/Group Leader at this scale. **No significant rank-vs-role tension internal to the role.** A few related dynamics:

- **You report to SFC Ferguson on 13 and 15 MAY** (when Ferguson is acting Branch Director). 2LT-reporting-to-SFC is normal in ICS Branch structure; the SECFOR billet runs through assignment.
- **You direct SSG Whalen as foot-patrol squad leader** — a 2LT directing an SSG by role. Whalen runs the squad on the ground; you run the screen-line element from the ICP. Coordinate; don't micromanage.
- **You're the 3 BN S4 by primary duty** — the dual-hat is light here (your S4 work doesn't conflict with screen-line leadership during the exercise window).

## Out of scope (do NOT do)

- **Do not commit the foot patrol inside the HAAP fence** — perimeter only.
- **Do not direct Air Ops** — coordinate, don't task.
- **Do not engage civilians beyond minimum courtesy** if you contact the foot patrol mid-shift.
- **Do not change the patrol position** without SECFOR Branch Director (Crosby) or Deputy (Ferguson) concurrence — the position is set by HAAP guidance.
