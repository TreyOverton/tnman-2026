# Planning Section Deputy

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Role** | Planning Section Deputy (per [ICS-203](../docs/ics-203.md) §5) |
| **Assignee** | **2LT Scott T. Sobel** (2 BN XO, MRC-1) |
| **Reports to** | Planning Section Chief — 1LT Overton |
| **Section** | Planning |
| **Operational period** | 13-15 MAY 26 |

## What this role is

The Planning Section Deputy supports the Planning Section Chief and covers the rail when the Chief is briefing the IC, running a planning meeting, or otherwise pulled away. The role:

- **Backs the Planning Chief** at the rail; takes the seat during the Chief's absences
- **Runs minor planning tasking** that doesn't need the Section Chief — coordinating Unit Leaders, chasing down ICS-214 stragglers, prepping the next planning meeting
- **Tracks Section deliverables** through the operational period
- **Bridges 2 BN visibility** into the TF planning effort — as 2 BN XO, you carry context the rest of the Section may not

## Expectations during TNMAN-26

### 13 MAY

- On-site by **0600** with the Planning Chief.
- Walk the **Planning Section setup** with 1LT Overton; verify boards, tables, forms, web portal access.
- Coordinate with the **Documentation Unit (CPT McCroskey)** on records intake.
- Run a quick **end-of-day Section sync** to capture lessons before close-out.

### 14 MAY

- **Continuous presence at the Planning rail.**
- Cover the rail during 1LT Overton's absences (briefing IC, running the planning meeting, stepping out).
- Track the **rolling ICS-214 stream** — make sure every Section is logging.
- During inject windows, coordinate with the **SITL (1SG Snow)** on picture updates and with the **Documentation Unit** on capture quality.
- Brief the Section Chief at any change in Section status.

### 15 MAY

- Cover the AAR-collation effort if 1LT Overton is briefing DIV / IC.
- Capture 2 BN-relevant AAR observations.

## Rank vs role

This is a notable rank dynamic. **2LT Sobel reports to 1LT Overton** — both are junior officers, but Sobel is rank-junior to Overton by one grade. That's a normal officer-track relationship.

The more interesting dynamic in this Section is who you *both* direct — a CPT (McCroskey), a 1SG (Snow), an SSG (Miles), and a WO1 (Hendon). The Planning Section runs entirely through ICS role assignment rather than rank line.

- **You step in for 1LT Overton** without diluting Section authority — the Section's direction carries the same weight whether the Chief or Deputy is at the rail.
- **You direct CPT McCroskey, 1SG Snow, etc., through the Deputy seat** when covering for Overton. Outside that context, normal rank deference applies.
- **You're dual-hatted as 2 BN XO** — the dual-hat is light here; 2 BN's organic activity during the exercise is minimal (BN CO, XO, HQ Co 1SG only).

## Out of scope (do NOT do)

- **Do not act for the Planning Chief on Section-direction-level decisions** without clear delegation. Cover the rail; don't redirect the section.
- **Do not run a Unit** — those are run by Unit Leaders (RUL, SITL, Documentation, Tech Specialists).
- **Do not duplicate the Resources Unit's personnel work** — they own the data; you consume it.
- **Do not engage external organizations** without IC / Liaison routing.
