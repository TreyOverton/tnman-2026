# Assistant Operations Specialist

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Role** | Assistant Ops Specialist (per [ICS-203](../docs/ics-203.md) §7, "Alternate" row) |
| **Assignee** | **SGT Tony W. Malone** (3 BN A Co MP, **MRC-3** — single-day participation on 14 MAY) |
| **Reports to** | Operations Section Chief — LTC Sheaf (and Ops Deputy — 1SG Collins) |
| **Section** | Operations |
| **Operational period** | 14 MAY 26 (single day per MRC-3 deviation) |

## What this role is

The Asst Ops Specialist supports the Operations Section in close-in activity tracking and tasking detail. The role:

- **Captures operational tasks and outcomes** as they happen — what was tasked, who took it, when it resolved
- **Runs minor tasking** that doesn't need a Branch Director (relays, coordination calls, escort assignments)
- **Backs the Asst Ops bench** during the busy parts of 14 MAY — multiple injects landing in close proximity
- **Bridges section-level and branch-level detail** — the Branch Directors and Section Chief shouldn't have to chase routine status

## Expectations during TNMAN-26

### 14 MAY (the only day)

- **On-site by 0600** with the Ops Section.
- Sit at the **Operations rail** with the Section Chief and Deputy.
- Capture each **inject** as it lands:
  - **1000** SECFOR patrol release
  - **1200** waterway breach (notional) and ICP IOC fully established
  - **1300** OPFOR main-gate scenario
  - **1400-1430** sUAS attack / detection / response
- Maintain a **personal ICS-214** through the day; mark `AAR:` entries in real time.
- Coordinate with the **Comms Unit (CSM Rutherford / SSG Singh)** on outbound radio traffic from the Operations rail.

### Single-day MRC-3 considerations

- You are **not on duty** on 13 or 15 MAY per the MRC-3 deviation (BG Cox concurrence required, [DD 2977 Block 4](../docs/dd2977.md)). The Ops Deputy (1SG Collins) covers Asst Ops functions on those days.
- **Hydration / heat awareness** is non-negotiable — single-day participation does not mean reduced safety attention. Coordinate with the **Safety Officer (SFC Sturgill)** if you feel any heat / fatigue effect.
- Bring your **ICS-214 hardcopy or laptop** at 0600 sign-in — once the web portal is live, hardcopy is fallback only.

## Rank vs role

SGT is appropriate rank for an Asst Ops Specialist role. The role is largely tasking detail and section logging — not a command position. **No significant rank-vs-role dynamic for this billet.**

One related dynamic: as an A Co MP NCO, you may be asked by **MAJ Crosby (SECFOR Branch Director)** for an MP-specific input. Default through the **Ops Section Chief** before responding to operational direction from outside the Section — Branch Directors should route through Sheaf for cross-Section taps.

## Out of scope (do NOT do)

- **Do not direct SECFOR positioning** — that's the SECFOR Branch Director.
- **Do not run radio traffic** — that's the Comms Unit.
- **Do not act for the Ops Section Chief or Deputy.** You support; they direct.
- **Do not skip MRC-3 hydration/heat protocols** — single-day participation requires the same attention.
