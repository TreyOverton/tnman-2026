# Service Branch Director

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Role** | Service Branch Director (per [ICS-203](/docs/ics-203.md) §6) |
| **Assignee** | **2LT Mark S. Neisler** (4 BN BN S4, MRC-4 — verification pending) |
| **Reports to** | Logistics Section Chief — CPT Haddix |
| **Section** | Logistics |
| **Operational period** | 13-15 MAY 26 |

## What this role is

The Service Branch Director runs the **services side of the Logistics Section** — the moving parts that aren't physical setup or comms or medical:

- **Hot-spot logistics** — when an inject lands, who needs water/snacks/batteries delivered to where
- **Vehicle coordination** — POV moves, the TACN-radio kit movement, any equipment shuttle
- **Charging strip rotation** — phones / radios / battery packs cycling through the multi-USB hub
- **Resupply coordination** — when the water station needs refill, when the snack table needs restock

The role exists to keep the Logistics Section responsive when CPT Haddix and SFC Bradley are tied up with macro-Section work.

## Expectations during TNMAN-26

### 13 MAY

- On-site by **0600**.
- Walk the **Logistics setup** with SFC Bradley; understand the layout, location of consumables, vehicle disposition.
- Brief CPT Haddix and SFC Bradley on the day's planned hot-spot logistics.

### 14 MAY

- **Continuous presence at the Logistics position.**
- During inject windows, **execute hot-spot logistics** — water to the screen position, batteries to Air Ops, charging-strip rotation at the ICP.
- Coordinate with **Comms Unit (CSM Rutherford / SSG Singh)** on radio swap-outs and battery rotation.
- Coordinate with the **Foot Patrol** on resupply timing (typically a midday water + snack cycle).
- Maintain ICS-214; flag AAR observations.

### 15 MAY

- Run **demobilization service tasks** — recovery of water containers, charging strip, signage; vehicle dispersal coordination.
- Coordinate with the **Staging Area Manager** on departure sequencing.

## Rank vs role

2LT is junior officer rank; this is appropriate for a Service Branch Director on a TF this size. **No significant rank-vs-role tension within the role.** Two related dynamics:

- **You're dual-hatted as 4 BN BN S4** — primary duty perspective is logistics-supply, which fits the role naturally.
- **You're MRC-4 — verification pending.** See [Roster Open Items](/roster.md). If MRC-4 status holds at execution, DD 2977 mitigations apply (ICP-interior posture).

## Out of scope (do NOT do)

- **Do not run the Comms Unit** — CSM Rutherford and SSG Singh own technical comms.
- **Do not run the Medical Unit** — MAJ Gampe owns medical.
- **Do not duplicate the Logistics Deputy's physical setup work** — Bradley owns baseline setup; you own the dynamic execution.
- **Do not freelance vehicle moves** without coordination with the Staging Area Manager.
