# Resources Unit Leader (RUL)

| Field | Value |

| **Reports to** | Planning Section Chief — 1LT Overton |
| **Section** | Planning |
| **Operational period** | 13-15 MAY 26 |

## What this role is

The Resources Unit Leader (RUL) owns **personnel and resource accountability** for the operational period:

- Maintains the **personnel-on-station roster** — who signed in, who's at staging, who's at the screen position, who's released
- **Verifies CalTopo dots** at sign-in alongside the Situation Unit
- Owns the **resource inventory** — radios issued, vehicles assigned, kit checked out
- **Produces ICS-203** for each operational period (you prepared the 13 MAY ICS-203 — 12 MAY 1400)
- Coordinates with the **Staging Area Manager (MAJ Rookard)** on sign-in / sign-out flow
- Backs the **Safety Officer (SFC Sturgill)** on personnel disposition during any incident

## Expectations during TNMAN-26

### 13 MAY

- On-site by **0530** to set up the sign-in station.
- **Run sign-in** at 0600 — verify identity, CalTopo dot, radio assignment, kit issued.
- Maintain the **ICS-211** (sign-in roster) for the operational period.
- Coordinate with the **Situation Unit (1SG Snow)** on CalTopo verification.
- Brief the Planning Chief on personnel-on-station status at the morning brief.
- **Prepare the 14 MAY ICS-203** with any updates from the day's reality (incoming LNOs, attached medics, etc.).

### 14 MAY

- **Continuous presence at the Planning rail.**
- Track personnel disposition through the day — staging, screen, ICP, released.
- Maintain **rolling ICS-211** updates as personnel move.
- During inject windows, support the **SITL** on personnel-related picture updates.
- During any incident, the RUL is the source of truth for "where is everyone" — feed the Safety Officer first.
- **Prepare the 15 MAY ICS-203.**

### 15 MAY

- Run sign-in for the half-day.
- Run **demobilization accountability** — every participant signs out and confirms departure.
- Hand the consolidated **ICS-211 set** to the Documentation Unit (CPT McCroskey).

## Rank vs role

1LT is appropriate rank for an RUL on a TF this size — the role doctrinally sits at SFC-CPT level. **No internal rank-vs-role tension within the role itself.** Two related dynamics:

- **You direct the Staging Area Manager (MAJ Rookard) on sign-in flow** — coordination, not command. Rookard runs staging; you set the data requirements he supports. This is a 1LT-coordinating-with-MAJ dynamic that runs through ICS role assignment, not rank.
- **You're MRC-2** — DD 2977 places you ICP-interior. The Staging Area Manager and Section Chiefs handle external interaction.

## Out of scope (do NOT do)

- **Do not run physical setup of staging** — that's the Staging Area Manager and Logistics.
- **Do not run the operational picture** — that's the SITL. You feed personnel data into their picture.
- **Do not duplicate the Documentation Unit's archival role** — you produce ICS-203 and ICS-211; they archive everything.
- **Do not engage external organizations** without IC / Liaison routing.
