## 1. Situation

**Area of Interest.** FEMA Region 4 (AL, FL, GA, KY, MS, NC, SC, TN); VA; AR; MO; the Mississippi River.

**Area of Operations.** The 26 counties of TEMA East Region and home-station / drill locations.

**Mt. Carmel Site Defense AO.** Vicinity of Holston Army Ammunition Plant (Plant A in Kingsport / Sullivan Co; Plant B in Hawkins Co; ~6,000 acres total; ~465 buildings).

**Enemy Forces.** UNK.

**Friendly Forces.** TNSG / TNNG / TEMA / Local Emergency Departments / Local Law Enforcement / TN Highway Patrol / multiple supporting states / Federal, State, and local agencies.

**Civil Considerations / Assumptions.** TBD.

## 2. Mission

3 RGT participates in DIV training operations in **TNMAN-26; Mt. Carmel Site Defense: a simulated kinetic attack**, occurring across TN **12-17 MAY 26** IOT increase MET proficiency and sustain collective and staff training proficiency at the Regimental level.

## Editor's Notes

- **Internal date inconsistency carried forward.** S3 Task 2 still references enclosures as "(22 APR 26)" while the actual enclosures are dated 30 APR 26 (and listed correctly in the ENCLS block at the bottom). Same kind of cosmetic discrepancy as FRAGO 1.
- **"Civilian support to military operations" appears verbatim** in S3 Task 5. This is the exact phrase the [JAG Pre-Brief — DSCA & TNSG Authority](/docs/jag-brief-dsca.md) asks JAG to clarify. Submitted pre-brief is open; FRAGO 2 retains the phrase pending JAG response.
- **Email typo.** Coordinating Instruction Task 6 lists CPT Haddix's email as `terrence.hadixx@tnstateguard.org` (double-x). Correct address per the [Mission Roster](/roster.md) is `terrence.haddix@tnstateguard.org`. Worth flagging when units act on the task.
- **TPFDD totals refined.** Encl C now shows **42-50 Estimated Actual / 73-83 Notional / 120-125 Estimated Total PAX** (varies hour-by-hour through 14 MAY) — finer-grained than the flat 54/70/124 split from the 23 APR TPFDD. Reflects more accurate placement of pilots and medics into ADVON / Notional / Actual. ADVON itself bumps from 11 → 25 (likely because adjacent-RGT pilots and medics now show under the ADVON line).
- **§107 sUAS now staffed: 5 TNSG + 5 IA pilots.** Encl C splits the §107 lane into TNSG pilots 1-5 and **IA (Inter-Agency) pilots 6-10**. Confirms the [Volunteer Pilots](/volunteer-pilots.md) integration; the RFF asked for 3 and Sheaf has 10 lined up.
- **Annex H Signal updates.** Compared to the prior 14 APR version, the current Annex H tightens the HF SHARES table format, fixes a date typo ("May 13th, 2025" → 2026 / Time Zone ROMEO), and updates the **Secondary HF Channel ECHO frequency from 9.295 MHZ → 6.8765 MHZ**.
