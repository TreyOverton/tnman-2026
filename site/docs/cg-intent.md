## CG's Mission Statement

> During the period of 13-17 May '26, the TN Stateguard (TNSG) participates in TN Maneuvers '26 across TN to Conduct Homeland Defense (HD) operations in response to ongoing attacks on the homeland, Improve TN Military Department damage assessment support capabilities, rehearse and train on emergency communications plans, in order to improve and demonstrate TNSG functional capabilities and to exercise the TN Emergency Response Plan.

## CG's Vision

Officers gaining a better understanding of:
- Incident Command Post establishment
- JBAT policies and regulations
- UAV/UAS Policy across State and Federal properties
- Developing multiple contacts from civilian emergency management counterparts at local levels

WO SMEs will increase Company Grade Officer, NCO, and enlisted expertise across functionalities including:
- Operational Concepts
- Troop leading procedures
- Pre-operational checks and inspections
- Recognizing the need to adapt to evolving branches and sequels

## CG's End State

> The TNSG's **3rd Regiment's successful establishment of an Incident Command Post** for conducting C3 and SECFOR Mission supporting Holston Army Ammunitions Plant, the TNSG G6 Cyber security team conducting TTX to the TNARNG G6 and Oak Ridge National Laboratory and the Y12 National Security Complex, and the TNSG Division Engineer Detachment attached to the JBAT conducting Bridge Damage Assessment will demonstrate TNSG capabilities in a Homeland Defense Scenario.

Establishing an ICP in Kingsport, TN with HF and TACN, providing ground and air (UAV) security with roving patrols to support and secure the Holston Army Ammunition Depot.

- Directly supported by 2nd and 4th Regiments and 31st MED Co
- **Establish ICP at the Kingsport Readiness Center within 6 hrs of notification**
- Establish communications with Holston Army Ammo Depot, the TNNG JOC through the TNSG LNO at the JOC, and conduct SECFOR operations as required with Depot Security
- **End State:** Well-developed and supportive perimeter ground and air security provided to the Holston Ammo Depot, and understanding policies, caveats, and efficient reporting to the JOC and TNSG Command Group
- Conduct DIRLAUTH with Holston POC (Mr. Tony Armstrong)
- Provide DIV Staff with RFFs over and above skills, capabilities, and capacities available
- Conduct DIV HF and TACN Radio COMMEX (1 per day, 13-15 May)
