## 1. Situation

**Area of Interest.** The states comprising FEMA Region 4 (AL, FL, GA, KY, MS, NC, SC, TN); VA; AR; MO; and the Mississippi River.

**Area of Operations.** The 26 counties that comprise TEMA East Region and home-station / drill locations.

**Holston Army Ammunition Plant.** Government-owned, contractor-operated ammunition and explosives plant; largest-capacity supplier of high-performance explosives in the country. The current plant is on two sites:

- **Plant A** — 4509 W Stone Dr, Kingsport, TN 37660 (Sullivan County) · 120 acres
- **Plant B** — ~4 miles (6.4 km) east in a less-developed part of Hawkins County · 5,900 acres

Plants A and B are connected by rail and include ~465 buildings total.

**Enemy Forces.** UNK.

**Friendly Forces.** TNSG / TNNG / TEMA / local emergency departments / local law enforcement / TN Highway Patrol / multiple supporting states / Federal, State, and local agencies.

**Interagency, Intergovernmental, and NGO.** TEMA Eastern Regional office provides technical guidance to county emergency managers, federal/state requirements for emergency management, NIMS reporting, training information, and a rapid avenue to request state or other external mutual aid. Major hazards include nuclear power / research / fuel / weapons facilities, wildland and forest fires, flooding, hazardous materials, severe weather, and earthquakes.
