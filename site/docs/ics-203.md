> *"Filling out the ICP leaves ~ four pax to almost fill out only one of the three 5-man Screen patrolling squads. So, we will have one 'almost actual' foot patrol and two notional on the screen line."* — LTC Sheaf, 29 APR

The actual squad is just smaller than originally projected.
