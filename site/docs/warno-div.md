## 2. Mission

The Division conducts TNMAN 13-17 May 2026 (Simulated Kinetic Attack — Holston Ammunition Plant) in multiple locations across TN during 4th Quarter, FY26 IOT increase proficiency, sustain collective training proficiency at the Regimental level, and increase staff proficiency at the Division and Regimental level.

## 3. Execution

### Initial Commander's Intent

This order is to synchronize the efforts of all units throughout the Division IOT maintain and improve readiness and METL-based training and Command and Control during TNMAN 26, and to sustain or improve the readiness of the TNSG.

### Concept of Operations

The TNSG will work with TNNG / TEMA / Local Emergency Departments / Local Law Enforcement / THP / Multiple States agencies to support the TNMAN 26 Simulated Kinetic Attack at Holston Ammunition Plant Scenario Operations.

**Decisive operation:** 61st MED BN support; 2nd Regiment Engineers bridge damage assessment and security force; 3rd Regiment SECFOR; 4th Regiment PODs mission; C2 OPS by Division and Regimental HQs; Radio Communications (600 / 100 Watt) conducted in multiple locations across the state.
