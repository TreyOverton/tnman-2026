## Scenario

A cyber event targeting **eagle-i.doe.gov** impacts TEMA operations. During the incident response, UTK is discovered to be a secondary target.

### Basic Scenario Timeline (Starting Scenario)

| Time (EST) | Event |
|------------|-------|
| 0800 | Eagle-I system admins notice strange activity and report to ORNL DCO of a potential cyber event |
| 0830 | ORNL DCO formally declares an incident |
| 0930 | TEMA is notified of degraded service to Eagle-I |
| 0945 | Known IOCs shared to State EOC |
| 1000 | UTK staff detects suspicious activity matching IOCs provided by ORNL |
| 1030 | UTK IMT/EOC responds to cyber attack and campus-wide infrastructure outage; UTK/ORNL attempt to determine joint staff list |
| 1130 | ORNL localized cell tower outage; TEMA notified via VoIP/email |
| 1140 | Eagle-I goes down entirely; TEMA notified |
| 1200 | ORNL requests radios from onsite EOC and coordinates channels with TEMA |
| 1205 | ORNL is offered Team Awareness Kit (TAK) to coordinate response |
| 1215 | Runner sent to pick up radios arrives |
| 1245 | ORNL achieves eradication and containment of cyber event |
| 1300 | Eagle-I service partially restored |
| 1400 | UTK reports partially restored continuity of operations |

### Injects

1. Eagle-I goes down entirely
2. After ORNL provides IOCs, UTK detects suspicious activity on their network
3. Localized cell tower outage; ORNL EOC brings out radios

## Objectives

1. Comms between ORNL and TEMA surrounding Eagle-I outage
2. LMR interop exercise / radio checks for a communication outage
3. State EOC intel sharing with UTK of IOCs / TTPs
4. IR tabletop exercise with ORNL / UTK

## Assumptions and Background Conditions

- Outside the scope of this scenario itself, federal communications are assumed to be restored via ECN.
- IJC3, FBI, and ORNL communications will be simulated on ORNL's side.
- ORNL will schedule a training session on Team Awareness Kits (TAK) — **@CW4 Simpson**.
- Once notified, UTK will follow its own response plan and generate its own injects for the remainder of the exercise.
- ORNL radios are initially only locally effective.

## Deliverables

- **ORNL** — Sharpen technical details with Eagle-I admins for the tabletop cyber event.
- **UTK** — Bring internal MSEL with internal cyber response actions.
- **TEMA** — Review plans/procedures with lead on how communications and resources will work between ORNL, UTK, etc.
- **TN State Guard** — Planning session for participation in this event.
