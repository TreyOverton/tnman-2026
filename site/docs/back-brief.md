# Orders Back-Brief — 3 RGT Expectations for TNMAN 26

> **Purpose:** Condense DIV OPORD 26-05, CG's Intent, and RGT OPORD 26-05-01 into a single one-page view of what 3 RGT is expected to do, who gives/receives support, and the hard NLTs. Useful as a read-ahead for the CG Operational Brief (18 APR) and for leader-level SA downward.

## 1. Mission (Nested)

| Level | Mission |
|-------|---------|
| **DIV** | The Division participates in TNMAN Operations **13-17 May 2026** to conduct Homeland Defense operations in response to ongoing attacks on the homeland across TN; improve TN Military Department damage assessment capabilities; rehearse and train on emergency communications plans; IOT improve/demonstrate TNSG functional capabilities and exercise the TN Emergency Response Plan. |
| **3 RGT** | 3 RGT participates in DIV training operations in TNMAN-26 (Simulated Kinetic Attack — Holston AAP) across multiple locations in TN **12-17 MAY 26** IOT increase MET proficiency and sustain collective and staff training proficiency at the Regimental level. |

## 2. CG's End State for 3 RGT

> "The TNSG's **3rd Regiment's successful establishment of an Incident Command Post** for conducting C3 and SECFOR Mission supporting Holston Army Ammunitions Plant ... will demonstrate TNSG capabilities in a Homeland Defense Scenario."

## 3. 3 RGT Key Tasks (from DIV OPORD 26-05, §3.i.4)

1. **Establish ICP at Armed Forces Reserve Center**, 399 A US-11W Scenic, Mt Carmel, TN 37645 (DIV OPORD wording: "Mt. Carmel Armory"), with HF and TACN. **Within 6 hrs of notification.** OIC: **COL Roark**.
2. **Provide ground and air (UAV) security** with roving patrols to support and secure the Holston Army Ammunition Depot.
3. **Provide DIV Staff with RFFs** for capabilities over and above 3 RGT organic skills. *(See [RFF/RFS](/docs/rff-rfs.md).)*
4. **Conduct DIV HF and TACN Radio COMMEX 13-15 May** (1 per day).
5. **Establish communications** with HAAP and the TNNG JOC (through TNSG LNO at JOC).
6. **Conduct SECFOR operations** as required with Depot Security. DIRLAUTH with Holston POC (Mr. Tony Armstrong).
7. **Provide sleeping area for 1st Regt SECFOR personnel** at the ICP. *(See Consistency Review — tension with CG dawn-to-dusk/RON-at-home; clarification requested.)*

## 4. Standing Constraints (CG Policy)

- **Dawn-to-dusk duty day.** No overnight duty.
- **RON at home** (or Soldier-funded hotel). No billeting at ICP for TNSG personnel.
- **Perimeter-only at HAAP.** No TNSG personnel enter HAAP premises; ICP sits outside the perimeter.
- **TNSG unarmed by default.** No Soldiers carry weapons for this mission.
- **AT is still the top priority.** Only CG/DCGO may authorize a Soldier to do TNMAN in lieu of AT.
- **IET requirement.** Soldiers who have not attended IET cannot attend TNMAN.
- **Unpaid status.** All Volunteers are in an unpaid status during TNMAN.
