## 2. Mission

3 RGT participates in DIV training operations in the Tennessee Maneuver Exercise 2026 (TNMAN-26) (Simulated Kinetic Attack — Holston Ammunition Plant) in multiple locations across TN 12-17 MAY 26 IOT increase MET proficiency, sustain collective and staff training proficiency at the Regimental level.

## 3. Execution

### Initial Commander's Intent

This order synchronizes the efforts of all units throughout the Regiment IOT maintain and improve readiness and METL-based training, and Command and Control during IDT, and to prepare for TNMAN-26.

### Concept of Operations

During TNMAN-26, 3 RGT will work with TNSG / TNNG / TEMA / Local Emergency Departments / Local Law Enforcement / TN Highway Patrol / multiple supporting states / Federal, State, and local agencies to support the TNMAN-26 (Simulated Kinetic Attack at Holston Ammunition Plant Scenario Operations).

**Decisive Operation:** SECFOR Operations — Area security; Dismounted and Aerial Patrols; Traffic Control Points (TCPs); Surveillance of the maritime domain (Holston River); and Radio Communications (600 / 100 Watt) conducted in multiple locations across the AO.

This operation will be conducted in three phases.

#### Phase I — Planning and Coordination

Begins with the receipt of Warning Order #1 to DIV OPORD 26-05 (TNMAN-26), 111100RFEB26. Consists of planning, interagency and unit coordinating actions, and notifications to support tasks and actions during Phase II. Ends with the receipt of tasking from authorized unit and agency representatives which initiate exercise training actions NET 120520RMAY26.

#### Phase II — Training Execution

Begins with the receipt of tasking from authorized unit and agency representatives which initiate exercise training actions NLT 120520RMAY26 (BMNT). Consists of (but not limited to) actions which alert, mobilize, and assemble TNSG personnel, resources, and capabilities at an Incident Command Post (ICP) or Resource Staging Area in vicinity of US Army Reserve Center, 399 US-11W Scenic, Mt Carmel, TN 37645, NLT 130519RMAY26 (BMNT).

**3 MP BN** is the Focus of Main Effort (FOME) for this phase, supported by 2 MP BN, 4 MP BN, HHC 3 RGT, and other supporting agencies and units. **3 MP BN** has the Priority of Support (POS), followed by HHC 3 RGT, 2 MP BN, and 4 MP BN respectively. FOME and POS remain in effect until changed as necessary and directed by the CDR / ICP.

Phase ends with notification to end the exercise (ENDEX) and to demobilize the assembled TNSG personnel, resources, and capabilities NLT 142131RMAY26 (EENT).

#### Phase III — Recovery and AAR

Begins NET 142131RMAY26 (end of Phase II). Consists of actions required to safely, efficiently, and effectively return TNSG personnel, resources, and capabilities to respective Home of Record, Home Station, and assigned units, and to a state of preparedness / readiness to respond to contingency / emergency events (if needed). Includes post-exercise activities such as Hot Wash, After Action Review and Reporting, and Corrective Action Planning and Programming in preparation for the next training event.

Initial actions during this phase will most likely be completed within approx. +24 Hrs of ENDEX (NLT 152131RMAY26); post-training administrative actions may potentially be completed +90 Days of ENDEX (approx. 10 AUG 26).
