## High-Priority Inconsistencies

### 1. Phase II end-time appears wrong in the RGT OPORD

**RGT OPORD §3:** "Phase II ends with the notification to end the exercise (ENDEX) and to demobilize the assembled TNSG personnel, resources, and capabilities **NLT 132131RMAY26 (EENT)**."

**DIV WARNO / RGT WARNO:** Exercise runs **13-17 May 2026**.

**[OPORD Enclosure A](docs/opord-encl-a.md):** Storyboard shows 13 MAY ADVON, **14 MAY main day**, **15 MAY half day + AAR**.

**[FPM slide deck](docs/fpm-slides.md):** State Guard track has 3 RGT in security roles on 14 MAY and 15 MAY.

**Finding:** The OPORD's stated Phase II ENDEX of 132131RMAY26 means 3 RGT's Phase II ends on the night of 13 MAY, after only ADVON/ICP-establishment day — before the main execution days. This is internally inconsistent with the storyboard, the FPM slide deck, and the DIV-level exercise window. Recommend changing to **152131RMAY26** (end of 3 RGT's half day + AAR) or **172131RMAY26** (end of full DIV window), depending on how much of the trailing exercise 3 RGT will participate in.

### 2. Mission-section date range mismatch

- **DIV WARNO §2 Mission:** "TNMAN 13-17 May 2026"
- **RGT WARNO §2 Mission:** "13-17 MAY 26"
- **RGT OPORD §2 Mission:** "**12-17 MAY 26**"

**Finding:** The OPORD expands the window to include 12 MAY (the day of assembly / receipt of tasking). This may be intentional if Phase II begin (120520RMAY26 BMNT) is meant to include staging, but the DIV-level mission statement is clearly 13-17 MAY. Recommend either: (a) align OPORD §2 to 13-17 MAY and describe 12 MAY movement as "pre-execution assembly"; or (b) keep 12 MAY and explicitly note the expanded window as a 3 RGT internal planning decision, not a DIV direction.

### 3. MOB Cell task dropped from RGT OPORD

**DIV WARNO:** "HHC G1 — Provide 2 PAX for MOB Cell at Sidco. MOB Cell at Sidco dates TBD. (Green, Yellow and Red Tabs)"

**RGT WARNO:** "S1 — During TNMAN-26, BPT to provide personnel for Mobilization (MOB) Cell support at Sidco, dates TBD." *(carried forward)*

**RGT OPORD:** **No mention of MOB Cell at Sidco.** OPORD S1 tasks are:
- Task 1: Provide a roster NLT 152000RAPR26
- Task 2: Coordinate personnel reporting with DIV G1
- Task 3: Provide Regimental Orders NLT 302000RAPR26
- Task 4: Track personnel during TNMAN-26
- Task 5: BPT account for attachment of personnel

**Finding:** The 2 PAX to Sidco MOB Cell task was in both the DIV WARNO and the RGT WARNO but was **dropped during the OPORD draft**. This is a live DIV tasking that must be executed. Recommend adding it back as **S1 Task 6** in the OPORD.

### 4. Medical attachment (61st MED BN) not reflected in RGT OPORD

**DIV WARNO:** "61st MED BN — Provide Medical Personnel dates and location TBD. (Green Tabs)"

**RGT WARNO / RGT OPORD:** No mention of a medical attachment or how 3 RGT will integrate the attached medics.

**Finding:** A verbal hint to 3 RGT indicated two medics may be attached. The DIV WARNO confirms that 61st MED BN is tasked to provide medical personnel DIV-wide. 3 RGT should (a) identify the expected number/composition of attached medics; (b) include an "Attached Forces" line in OPORD §1 Task Organization; (c) add a task to S3 to coordinate with 61st MED BN (CO: BG Cox) on dates/location; and (d) add a task to S4 for medical billeting / transportation support.

### 5. DIV WARNO 3 RGT SECFOR specifics not carried into RGT WARNO

**DIV WARNO:** Explicitly tasks 3 RGT with SECFOR missions including "area security, foot and drone (UAV) patrols of potential enemy OP's, TCP, Specifically the Holston River, UAV."

**RGT WARNO:** Subordinate tasks to 2 MP BN, 3 MP BN, 4 MP BN all say **"TBD."** None of the DIV-specified task elements are passed to any 3 RGT subordinate.

**RGT OPORD:** Correctly translates the SECFOR mission elements into specific BN tasks (SECFOR at HAAP, sUAS recon, convoy planning, ICP support, FAA Part 107 pilot from 4 BN).

**Finding:** The RGT WARNO missed the opportunity to pass DIV guidance one level down. The draft OPORD fixes this, so no OPORD action is required — but worth noting in any lessons-learned review of the warning order.

## Area of Operations Discrepancy

**DIV WARNO §1 AO:** "East TN" (simple, correct for the actual mission).

**RGT WARNO / RGT OPORD §1 AO:** "The 26 counties that comprise TEMA East Region; Volunteer Training Site – Tullahoma (VTS-T); Volunteer Training Site – Smyrna (VTS-S); Volunteer Training Site – Milan (VTS-M); and Home Station / Drill Locations."

**Finding:** VTS-T is in Middle Tennessee (Tullahoma); VTS-S is Middle Tennessee (Smyrna); VTS-M is West Tennessee (Milan). Including all three in a 3 RGT AO for a HAAP / Mt Carmel mission is inaccurate — the RGT docs appear to inherit boilerplate AO language from a broader training order. Recommend scoping the RGT AO to TEMA East Region, the RSA/ICP at Mt Carmel, HAAP, and Home Stations — or align directly with DIV's "East TN."

## Minor / Housekeeping

- **Greenville, TN** — Both RGT WARNO and RGT OPORD headers read "Greenville, TN" (missing the second "e"). Correct spelling is **Greeneville**. *(Already flagged to LTC Sheaf separately.)*
- **DTG format** — DIV WARNO header reads "111100RFEBRUARY2026"; standard DTG format is "111100RFEB26." Both RGT docs use the correct format.
- **DIV WARNO city casing** — DIV WARNO header reads "Nashville, tn" (lowercase). Minor cosmetic issue at the DIV level.
- **Reference list divergence** — DIV WARNO lists AR 350-1, FM 5-0, ADP 3-0/5-0/6-0/7-0, STP 21-1-SMCT, VTS SOPs, and the TNNG LOI. RGT WARNO lists only "WO 1 to DIV OPORD 26-05." RGT OPORD lists WO 1, BG Stewart 30 MAR email, and RGT WARNO 26-05-01. This is normal for a subordinate order (references inherited from higher), but LTC Sheaf may want to cite the TNNG LOI directly in the OPORD since it drives several mission-specific constraints.
- **Commander's Intent — prepare for itself** — RGT OPORD Initial Commander's Intent reads "...during IDT and **prepare for TNMAN-26**." Since TNMAN-26 *is* the operation described by the OPORD, preparing for it is implicit. Minor phrasing nit.
- **EN DET and PMO tasks** — DIV WARNO HHC tasks include EN DET bridge damage assessment and PMO TNSG-TOC security. Neither applies to 3 RGT HHC (no engineer detachment; no TOC security mission). Correctly omitted from the RGT documents; noting for completeness.
- **1st Regiment / 4th Regiment lines** — DIV WARNO tasks 1st Regiment (backfill) and 4th Regiment (PODs) — these are peer regiments, not subordinates of 3 RGT. Correctly omitted from RGT documents.

## Recommendations to LTC Sheaf

Before finalizing the OPORD, I recommend:

1. **Fix Phase II ENDEX** (132131RMAY26 → 152131RMAY26 or 172131RMAY26). *High priority — current wording has 3 RGT demobilizing before the main execution days.*
2. **Reconcile the mission date range** — either 12-17 MAY with a note, or 13-17 MAY with 12 MAY called out as pre-execution assembly.
3. **Reinstate the MOB Cell at Sidco task** under S1 (Task 6).
4. **Add a line for the 61st MED BN attachment** in Task Organization and add S3/S4 coordination tasks.
5. **Scope the AO correctly** — drop VTS-T/S/M from RGT §1 unless there is a reason to keep them.
6. **Consider citing the TNNG LOI** directly in the OPORD references.

None of these are blockers for routing the draft for command comment, but all are improvements that will make the OPORD consistent with DIV guidance and with the exercise as it is actually going to run.
